You're skeletally mature. You can train hard. You can also injure yourself in ways an eighteen-year-old can't. This program accounts for both.

This is a real off-season power and speed plan for adult hockey players across the 19-39 age range — built to the same coaching standard used with senior pro and national-team athletes, with dedicated scaling for the 33+ sub-group whose connective tissue needs more time to recover than their muscles do.

Twelve weeks. Forty-eight sessions. About 65-75 minutes each. Bodyweight and bands only — no gym, no plates. That's not a compromise; it's a design choice. Variable band tension and the contrast methods built into this program are arguably better suited to power-speed development than fixed loads.

Inside the program

What's included.

  • 01
    3 four-week blocks

    Foundation → Specific Prep → Power Realization. Built on block periodization.

  • 02
    Six-block session architecture every session

    RAMP · Primer · Power · Strength · Energy Systems · Cool-Down. Structured for transfer.

  • 03
    Reactive plyometrics, max-velocity flying sprints, hill sprints, contrast/complex training

    The full elite toolkit, made executable with bodyweight + bands.

  • 04
    8-test combine battery including 30m fly sprint and RSI

    Reactive strength index. Track measurable progress every 4 weeks.

  • 05
    Dedicated AGE SCALING page with three columns

    19-25 high tolerance · 26-32 moderate · 33-39 managed loading. 9 specific modifications.

  • 06
    The hockey-specific non-negotiables

    Adductor work twice weekly · single-leg ≥40% lower-body volume · rotational power every week · anti-rotation core every session.

  • 07
    Recovery, mindset, & lifestyle protocols

    Because at 30+, recovery is half the program. Sleep, stress, training-life balance.

  • 08
    Full exercise glossary, progression/regression library

    Every exercise documented with options to match your level on any given day.

Realistic expected gains across twelve weeks:

4-8 cm vertical jump · 15-25 cm broad jump · 0.10-0.25 sec on 10m sprint · 0.05-0.15 sec on 30m fly · 15-25% RSI gain · measurably more powerful, more durable, harder to play against.

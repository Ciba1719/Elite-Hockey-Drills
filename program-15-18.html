Inside the program

What's included.

  • 01
    3 four-week blocks

    Foundation → Specific Prep → Power Realization. Built on block periodization, not random workouts.

  • 02
    Six-block session architecture

    RAMP · Primer · Power · Strength · Energy Systems · Cool-Down. Every session, every time.

  • 03
    Reactive plyometrics, resisted sprints, contrast/complex training

    The methods that actually move the needle on hockey-specific speed.

  • 04
    7-test combine battery with mid-block checkpoints

    Vertical jump, broad jump, 10m sprint, 5-10-5 pro-agility, single-leg symmetry, Copenhagen, Pallof iso.

  • 05
    The non-negotiables for hockey transfer

    Adductor work twice weekly · single-leg ≥40% of lower-body volume · anti-rotation core every session.

  • 06
    Recovery & lifestyle protocols

    Sleep, nutrition, mindset — because the work that doesn't get done off-the-page is what costs you the gains.

  • 07
    Full exercise glossary & progression library

    Every exercise documented, every progression mapped.

  • 08
    Sub-maximal testing only

    No 1RM, no max-velocity sprints, no joint risks you don't need. Smart development, not reckless training.

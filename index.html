02 — The Method

Built on science.
Tested by hockey.

Most off-ice programs are recycled gym templates. Mine are designed from the ground up around how hockey players actually move, fatigue, and produce force.

i.

Sport Science

Every exercise selection is grounded in evidence — biomechanics, energy systems, fatigue management. No guessing, no trends. Just what's been shown to work.

ii.

Periodization

Programs are structured in progressive blocks — strength, power, peak — so you build correctly across the season instead of chasing fatigue.

iii.

Hockey-Specific

Built around the demands of the sport — explosive starts, lateral power, rotational strength, repeat-sprint ability. Generic fitness doesn't translate. This does.

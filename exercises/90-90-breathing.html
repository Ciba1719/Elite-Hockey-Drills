Cool-down / Recovery

90/90 Breathing

Diaphragmdeep core Aerobic
i.

How to Do It

Lie on your back with your feet flat on a chair, knees and hips bent to 90 degrees. Rest your hands on your ribs. Inhale slowly through the nose, feeling the ribs expand, then exhale long and fully through the mouth. Continue for the prescribed time.

ii.

Why It Works

The 90/90 position parks the pelvis and ribs so the diaphragm can do the breathing, downshifting the nervous system.

iii.

Hockey Transfer

It is the deliberate recovery that lets you adapt to training and come back fresh next session.

iv.

Coaching Cues

  • "In through the nose, long slow exhale."
v.

Common Mistakes

Breathing into the chest and shoulders; rushing the exhale; arching the back.

vi.

Progression / Regression

Progression

Lengthen the exhale (in 4, out 8).

Regression

Shorten the duration or the breath count.

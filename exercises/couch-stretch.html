Cool-down / Recovery

Couch Stretch

Hip flexorsquads Aerobic
i.

How to Do It

Place your back foot up on a couch or chair, shin vertical, with your front foot forward in a tall lunge. Squeeze the back glute hard and stand tall through the back hip. You will feel a strong stretch down the front of the back-leg hip and thigh.

ii.

Why It Works

It targets the hip flexors and quads that shorten from sitting and skating, restoring the hip extension a powerful stride needs.

iii.

Hockey Transfer

Open hip flexors mean a longer stride and a back that is not forced to compensate.

iv.

Coaching Cues

  • "Squeeze the back glute. Stay tall."
v.

Common Mistakes

Arching the lower back to fake more range; collapsing forward.

vi.

Progression / Regression

Progression

Sink deeper or hold longer.

Regression

Lower the back foot or reduce knee flexion.
